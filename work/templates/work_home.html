Freedom COOP

Welcome to the Online Collaborative Platform



To start using the OCP:

  • First, you need an account on the system. Talk to a Freedom Coop administrator if you don't have one.
  • You should go to the My Profile page and update your information and register your skills.
  • You can also add yourself to the Map page.
  • After you are set up, you can log your work in either the Non-process Logging or the Process Logging page, depending on the type of work or project you are involved with.

You can also find help on each page, in the upper right corner.
